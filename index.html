Tune Out is a music player that lets you hear what's going on around you. It activates your iPhone/iPod Touch's built-in microphones and optionally pauses or turns down your music so that you can listen to your surroundings without taking off your headset. Perfect for:


    - Keeping track of your stop while enjoying your music on the bus or subway

    - Staying alert while running or biking outside in urban environments

    - Listening to music in the office without ignoring your coworkers

Tune Out is the simplest, most convenient awareness-enhancing app in the marketplace. It's the first app that combines ambient noise-monitoring capabilities with a fully-functional music player. With Tune Out, you only need a single app to set up your ideal listening environment. You can quickly enable or disable your device's microphone without even touching your screen!

FAQ

What devices/operating systems are supported by Tune Out?
Tune Out currently supports iPhone and iPod Touch devices running iOS 7.0 or above.
Can I use streaming service such as Spotify with Tune Out?
Tune Out out is designed to work best with the iTunes library on your device. You can use the Aware Mode with other music apps, however you will not be able to control Tune Out with your headphone controls when using another music app.
Do I need headphones with a inline microphone and controls?
No, Tune Out can use your iPhone/iPod Touch's built-in microphone to access the Aware Mode functionality. We do recommend a headset with inline controls as this enables you to activate Aware Mode without even touching your screen, but any headset will work.
